About Our Company

Established in 1994, A.D. Enterprises has thirty years of experience in manufacturing of Piezo Ringers, Piezo Buzzers and Electronic Push Button Telephones. Annual outputs of these products has reached 0.5 million pieces.

In addition to advanced facilities and skilled workers, our company also has a team of highly innovative engineers who are capable of designing and developing various state-of-the-art Piezo Ringers, Piezo Buzzers. OEM and ODM projects have always been welcomed.

Our products had been approved by Department of Telecommunication (CACT, Govt. of India) and also Electronic Testing and Development Corporation (ETDC, Govt. of Gujarat). Company has also been rewarded Zed Certification: Link to the Zed Certification.

And company has also been registered as MSME with Govt. of Gujarat: Link to the MSME Certification.

The watch word of our company is To have a lot of Satisfied Customers backed by excellent Quality, Competitive Price, Prompt Delivery and our commitment to after Sales & Service.

Your Inquiries will be warmly accommodated.
